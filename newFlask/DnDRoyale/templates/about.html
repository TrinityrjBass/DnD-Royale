T HIS online tool calculates the victory probabilities in a D&D encounter which simulates an encounter 1,000 times for statistical accuracy. The combatants can be selected from a list with their original stat blocks as found in the Monster Manual, or the user can choose to modify the stat block.
The simulator is based on the 5e rules in how it handles crits, conditions, damage types, advantages and disadvantages.

Technical notes

Limitations

  • There's no concept of space, so there's no range or movement. AOEs assume at least 50% of possible targets are targeted.
  • No magical spells (it's on the todo list)
  • Not all abilities are added (work in progress)
  • Social abilities will not be added (no value to combat)
  • Custom Attack tool is not very intuitive (on the todo list)
  • Power graph can be very misleading (on the todo list)
  • There's no legendary actions
  • PC's can't be used
  • There's no magical items
  • No status conditions (on the todo list)

Section help

Simulation Settings


Some optional rules have been added under "Simulation Options".
  • Morale is determined based on the creature's CR. Certain events happen during battle that effect the creature's morale, such as falling under 1/2 of their total HP (becoming 'bloodied') and getting healed by an ally.
  • Attack Damage Type enables resistances, immunities, and vulnerabilities to all damage types including magical types.
    If you would like to use any of these optional settings, check the box next to the setting that you would like to use in your simulation.
